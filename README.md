## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate specializing in **control systems, optimization, and learning-based decision-making** for autonomous and cyber-physical systems. My work focuses on combining **Model Predictive Control (MPC)** and **Deep Reinforcement Learning (SAC, TD3, DDPG)** to design **robust, energy-efficient, and safety-aware control systems**.

My research addresses **real-world challenges in autonomous driving**, including:
- Communication packet loss  
- Uncertain or delayed preview information  
- Real-time feasibility of non-convex optimization
